A beginner-friendly HTML &amp; CSS template to help you build your personal portfolio website in 3 hours!

### Step 1: Download and Setup
1. Download this entire folder to your computer
2. Open the folder in your text editor (VS Code, Sublime Text, etc.)

### Step 2: Open Your Website
1. Find the `index.html` file in the folder
2. Right-click it and choose "Open with" ‚Üí Your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
3. Your portfolio will open in the browser!

### Step 3: Edit Your Content
1. Open `index.html` in your text editor
2. Look for comments that start with `<!-- ‚úÖ CHANGE THIS:`
3. Replace the example text with your own information from your CV
4. Save the file (Ctrl+S or Cmd+S)
5. Refresh your browser (F5) to see the changes


1. **Save often** - Press Ctrl+S (or Cmd+S on Mac) frequently
2. **Refresh to see changes** - Press F5 in your browser after saving
3. **One change at a time** - Make small changes and test them
4. **Ask for help** - Don't hesitate to ask the instructor or a neighbor!
5. **Keep your CV handy** - You'll copy information from it


**How to change colors:**
1. Open `style.css`
2. Find the `:root` section (lines 11-18)
3. Replace the color codes (e.g., `#2563eb`) with your own
